<h1>Clinical Research</h1>

<p>Our clinical research develops multimodal imaging methods to study bone growth, development, repair, and joint health in pediatric populations affected by chronic and complex diseases.</p>

</div>

{% include section.html %}

## Current Directions

- Longitudinal HR-pQCT imaging of pediatric fracture healing
- Advanced imaging approaches for juvenile idiopathic arthritis
- Bone strength, body composition, and growth in childhood obesity

## Methods

This stream combines high-resolution imaging, clinical cohorts, computational modelling, and measures of growth, muscle, and function to better understand pediatric bone health.
