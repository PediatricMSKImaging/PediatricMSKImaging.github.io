<p>We welcome graduate students, postdocs, and summer students who are excited about pediatric imaging, biomechanics, computation, and bone health.</p>

If you are interested in joining the lab, please email your CV/resume, an unofficial transcript, a short description of your research interests, and whether you are most interested in computational, imaging, clinical, or experimental work.

We especially encourage applicants from engineering, computer science, biological sciences, biomedical sciences, and biomechanics backgrounds to get in touch.

## Current Opportunities

Specific funded roles or job postings can be added here when they become available.

For now, we welcome general inquiries from prospective trainees and collaborators who are interested in the lab.

## What We Value

- Curiosity about pediatric musculoskeletal health
- Interest in collaborative, interdisciplinary research
- Experience or enthusiasm in imaging, data analysis, biology, engineering, or biomechanics
